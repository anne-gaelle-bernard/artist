Savage X Fenty

Rihanna co-owns Savage X Fenty, a lingerie line disrupting norms and celebrating diversity since its spring 2018 inception. Despite stepping down as CEO in 2023, Rihanna remains as executive chair, ensuring her continued guidance in shaping the brand's vision. With the appointment of Hillary Super as CEO, Rihanna's strategic move allows her to focus on her creative leadership while exploring new opportunities in music, beauty, and beyond.

Fenty Beauty

Rihanna's entrepreneurial journey spans the fashion and cosmetics industries, with her iconic ventures redefining standards of beauty and empowerment by offering a diverse range of products suitable for all skin tones. From the groundbreaking launch of Fenty Beauty, achieving remarkable revenue of $570 million in 2019, to the historic moment of founding Fenty, marking her as the first woman and black woman to join LVMH, Rihanna continues to challenge norms and inspire inclusivity.

Fenty Skin

Fenty Skin, Rihanna's highly anticipated skincare brand, revolutionizes the industry with its inclusive approach, catering to individuals of all skin tones and types. Emphasizing simplicity and effectiveness, the debut trio of products offers a streamlined skincare routine while incorporating potent ingredients like Barbados cherry and hyaluronic acid. With sleek, refillable packaging and a sensorial experience infused with tropical fragrances, Fenty Skin elevates self-care to new heights, embodying Rihanna's commitment to accessible and luxurious skincare for all.
